What does a lotus symbolize?

The lotus flower is regarded in many different cultures,especiallyin eastern religions,as a symbol of purity,enlightenment,self-regeneration and rebirth.

Interesting facts about lotus flower

  1. Lotus can reach only 49 inches in height and 10 feet in width.
  2. Lotus flowers are found in white, pink, red, blue, purple and yellow.
  3. Lotus is even believed to have given birth to sun.
  4. Flower, young leaves, seeds and roots are edible and oflen used in Asian cuisine.
  5. Lotus can survive thousands of year and even to revive after long period of dromancy.
  6. Dried stamens of lotus are used for the preparation of aromatic tea.
  7. Korea, China, and Japan all have lotus festivals, celebrating the beauty of this flower.
  8. The lotus flower is a very popular tattoo

What colours of lotus means what?

  1. BLUE~It is associated with a victory of the spirit over the wisdom, intelligence and knowledge.
  2. WHITE~Symbolizes Bodhi and represents a state of mental purity, and that of spiritual perfection.
  3. PINK~This is the supreme lotus and is considered to be the true lotus of Buddha.
  4. YELLOW~It represents achievement and enlightenment
  5. RED~This colour of lotus is regarded to heart , love and compassion.
  6. PURPLE~The eight petals of lotus flower are representative of the noble eightfold path.
